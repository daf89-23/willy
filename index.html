About


The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC) is an institution located in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Established in 1965, it serves as a regional center of excellence for statistical training and capacity building in Eastern Africa. Here are some key points about EASTC:
1.Mission: EASTC's mission is to enhance the capacity of countries in Eastern Africa in statistical development through training, research, and consultancy services.
2.Training Programs: EASTC offers a wide range of training programs in statistics and related fields. These programs cater to various levels of expertise, from basic statistical methods to advanced techniques. Courses cover topics such as survey design and implementation, data analysis, statistical software applications, and data visualization.
3.Regional Focus: As a regional institution, EASTC primarily serves the countries of Eastern Africa, including Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, and the Democratic Republic of Congo. Its training programs are tailored to address the specific statistical needs and challenges faced by these countries.
4.Partnerships: EASTC collaborates with various international organizations, development partners, and academic institutions to deliver high-quality training and support. Partnerships with organizations such as the United Nations Statistical Institute for Asia and the Pacific (UNSIAP), the World Bank, the African Development Bank (AfDB), and the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa (UNECA) help EASTC stay updated with global best practices and trends in statistical capacity building.
5.Consultancy Services: In addition to training programs, EASTC provides consultancy services to governments, national statistical offices, and other organizations in the region. These services may include assistance with survey design, data analysis, capacity assessments, and technical support for statistical projects.
